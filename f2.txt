The apple is one of the most grown tree fruits. It is grown in orchards.The apple tree came from Central Asia. Its wild ancestor is still there today. China produced about 35% of the world's apples.[3] The United States produces more than 7.5% of the apples around the world. This makes it the second greatest producer.
